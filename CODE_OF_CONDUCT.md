# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as maintainers and contributors, pledge to make participation in this project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.  
We commit to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an open, safe, diverse, and inclusive environment.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful, considerate, and constructive
- Giving and gracefully accepting feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission
- Any conduct that could be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards.  
They will take corrective action that they deem appropriate for any behavior they consider inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies:

- Within project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, code reviews)
- In public spaces where an individual is representing the project or its community

## Enforcement Guidelines
Maintainers will follow these guidelines when determining consequences:

### 1. Correction
**Consequence:** A private reminder about expected behavior.

### 2. Warning
**Consequence:** A formal warning and consequences for continued behavior.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Consequence:** A temporary ban from project interactions.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Consequence:** A permanent ban from the project and community spaces.
